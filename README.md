> A curated list of tools, platforms, policies, and projects that enable digital transformation in public services and governance.

E-Government (or digital government) refers to the use of digital technologies to deliver public services, engage citizens, and improve the efficiency, transparency, and accountability of government institutions.

## General Resources

- [UN E-Government Survey](https://publicadministration.un.org/egovkb/en-us/) – Comprehensive global assessment of digital government readiness.
- [OECD Digital Government](https://www.oecd.org/gov/digital-government/) – Resources and analysis on public sector digital transformation.
- [GovStack Initiative](https://www.govstack.global/) – A collaborative effort to standardize digital government building blocks.

## E-Government Platforms

- [Estonia e-Residency](https://e-resident.gov.ee/) – Access Estonia’s digital infrastructure as a global citizen.
- [IndiaStack](https://www.indiastack.org/) – Digital infrastructure for identity, payments, and data empowerment in India.
- [gov.uk](https://www.gov.uk/) – Unified public service delivery portal for the United Kingdom.
- [USAGov](https://www.usa.gov/) – Centralized federal service platform for U.S. citizens.

## Policy & Strategy

- [Digital Nations Charter](https://www.digitalnations.net/) – Shared vision of digital government by leading countries.
- [EU Digital Government Factsheets](https://joinup.ec.europa.eu/collection/nifo-national-interoperability-framework-observatory/digital-government-factsheets) – National digital policy summaries for EU states.
- [Canada’s Digital Government Strategy](https://www.canada.ca/en/government/system/digital-government/government-canada-digital-operations-strategic-plan-2021-2024.html) – Strategic plan for federal digital operations.

## Case Studies & Global Initiatives

- [World Bank GovTech Initiative](https://www.worldbank.org/en/topic/governance/brief/govtech-putting-people-first) – Support for digital transformation of government.
- [Digital Public Goods Alliance](https://digitalpublicgoods.net/) – Promoting open-source solutions for digital public infrastructure.
- [UNDP Digital Strategy](https://digitalstrategy.undp.org/) – Advancing digital transformation for sustainable development.

## Open Data & APIs

- [Data.gov](https://www.data.gov/) – U.S. government’s open data portal.
- [European Data Portal](https://data.europa.eu/en) – Access public sector information across the EU.
- [Canada Open Government](https://open.canada.ca/en) – Government data, APIs, and transparency tools for Canada.

## Digital Identity & Authentication

- [Gov.uk Verify](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/introducing-govuk-verify) – UK digital identity verification service.
- [eIDAS](https://ec.europa.eu/digital-building-blocks/wikis/display/DIGITAL/eIDAS) – EU regulation for electronic identification and trust services.
- [MOSIP](https://www.mosip.io/) – Modular open source identity platform for national digital ID systems.

## Citizen Engagement

- [Decidim](https://decidim.org/) – Free open-source platform for participatory democracy and citizen engagement.
- [CONSUL Democracy](https://consulproject.org/en/) – Digital platform for civic participation and governance.
- [CitizenLab](https://www.citizenlab.co/) – Civic tech platform to collect and prioritize community feedback.

## E-Services & Digital Service Delivery

- [Gov.uk Notify](https://www.notifications.service.gov.uk/) – Communication service for UK public sector.
- [Gov.sg](https://www.gov.sg/) – Singapore’s integrated public services and citizen communication portal.
- [Service Canada](https://www.canada.ca/en/employment-social-development/corporate/portfolio/service-canada.html) – One-stop access to Canadian government programs and services.

## Procurement & E-Governance Tools

- [Open Contracting Partnership](https://www.open-contracting.org/) – Open standards and tools for transparent procurement.
- [Procurement for the Public Good](https://www.oecd.org/gov/public-procurement/) – OECD tools and resources on fair and efficient procurement.
- [OpenGov](https://opengov.com/) – Cloud-based budgeting, performance, and communications for governments.
